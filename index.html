A little about myself

My name is Michael Coombs, and I am currently in my first year of computer science at Holland College located in Prince Edward Island, Canada. Since starting the CS program, I have found the amazing world of programming, database managment, web development, and more.

Creative

Over the years I have fostered a strong entrepreneurial spirit, from everything between woodworking to dropshipping with Shopify. I love being able to use my skill of having new ideas to create an innovate.

Health & Fitness

A strong passion of mine has always been health and fitness. Wether that be my own, or helping others find their path to a healthy lifestyle through exercise, nutrition, and mindset.

Sociology

Before starting my CS program, I had studied Sociology at the University of Prince Edward Island. This is where I developed a passion for the sociological aspects of marketing and UX/UI design.

Blockchain

Where do I see myself in 10 years? I see myself having a role in the innovative technology of blockchain. I currently enjoy following the progress of blockchain integration to our world, and teaching myself the basics along the way.

Skills
